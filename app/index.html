who i am?

I’ve been drawing since before I even learned to swim which is saying a lot considering I grew up in Hawaii. Since leaving the Islands’ beautiful beaches and landing in Portland, Oregon I’ve spent countless hours perfecting my craft as a professional illustrator. I look forward to spending more time bringing my imagination to your projects.
